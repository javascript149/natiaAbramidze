“Teamwork is more than just work”

Inspiring people to discover the world, is what moves us every day. We believe everyone should have the opportunity to work abroad because it is much more than just working abroad!

Together we are strong!

BUSIN is about more than just working. Inside and outside the organisation we are connected by the BUSINS culture. Our strong team spirit comes from working out together, having a drink together and making the world a little better every day. This gives us the energy to work towards our common goal: to inspire as many people as possible to discover the world.

Holacracy

At BUSIN there are no managers. There is no top-down structure and we do not have a traditional hierarchical division of our employees.The organisation is built on self-management and trust, because we work on the basis of Holacracy. Everyone is responsible for his or her own circles, roles, tasks and processes. At BUSIN we are all entrepreneurs in our own roles!
